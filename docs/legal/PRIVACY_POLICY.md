# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: [DATE]**

This Privacy Policy explains what data Slonbelka ("we," "us," "our") collects, how we use it, and your rights. Slonbelka is operated by [YOUR NAME] as a sole proprietor.

## 1. Data We Collect

- **Account data**: email address, hashed password (Argon2), email verification status.
- **Usage data**: review history, SRS scheduling state, accuracy stats, streaks, session summaries.
- **Device/technical data**: IP address, browser type, and similar metadata collected automatically for security and rate limiting.
- **Payment data**: handled entirely by Stripe. We receive subscription status and billing metadata, not full card numbers.
- **Offline queue data**: reviews answered offline are stored locally on your device until synced.

We do not collect data beyond what's needed to run lessons, reviews, sync progress, and process billing.

## 2. How We Use Data

- To operate core features: SRS scheduling, grading, dashboards, offline sync.
- To authenticate you and protect accounts (rate limiting, refresh tokens).
- To send transactional emails (verification, password reset, billing receipts).
- To process payments through Stripe.
- To fix bugs and understand aggregate usage; we don't sell your data or use it for third-party advertising.

## 3. Third-Party Services

We use the following processors, each with their own privacy practices:
- **Stripe** — payment processing.
- **Railway** — backend hosting.
- **Vercel** — frontend hosting.
- **Cloudflare R2** — audio file storage.

These providers process data on our behalf under their own data processing agreements; we don't control their independent use of data outside that relationship.
